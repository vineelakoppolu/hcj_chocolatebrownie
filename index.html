ingredients

To make a batch of rich and fudgy chocolate brownies, you’ll need 1/2 cup (115g) of melted unsalted butter,which provides the necessary moisture and richness. Combine it with 1 cup (200g) of granulated sugar and 1/2 cup (100g) of packed brown sugar for the perfect balance of sweetness and depth of flavor. You'll also need 2 large eggs, brought to room temperature, to help bind the ingredients together. Enhance the flavor with 2 teaspoons of vanilla extract. For the dry ingredients, sift together 3/4 cup (95g) of all-purpose flour, 1/2 cup (50g) of unsweetened cocoa powder, 1/2 teaspoon of salt, and 1/4 teaspoon of baking powder. If you like a bit of extra texture, you can fold in 1/2 cup (90g) of semi-sweet chocolate chips or chunks, and 1/2 cup (60g) of chopped walnuts or pecans for a delightful crunch.

PROCESS

To make brownies, start by preheating your oven to 350°F (175°C) and greasing your baking pan. Melt 1/2 cup (115g) of butter and mix it with 1 cup (200g) of granulated sugar and 1/2 cup (100g) of brown sugar. Stir in 2 eggs and 2 teaspoons of vanilla extract. In another bowl, combine 3/4 cup (95g) of flour, 1/2 cup (50g) of cocoa powder, 1/2 teaspoon of salt, and 1/4 teaspoon of baking powder. Add these dry ingredients to the wet mixture and stir until combined. Optionally, mix in 1/2 cup (90g) of chocolate chips and 1/2 cup (60g) of chopped nuts. Pour the batter into the pan and bake for 20-25 minutes. Let the brownies cool before cutting them into squares.
